HI THERE!
I’m Irene, a detail-oriented UX designer with a fascination for solving complex problems and structuring digital content. I believe that my role is to bridge the gap between users and digital systems by building efficient and user-centered experiences that help people accomplish their goals quickly and easily.
Outside of design, I love staying active through strength training and exploring new places. I enjoy seeing things from a fresh perspective and sharing experiences with people who have the same curiosity for life.
I’m always looking for opportunities to collaborate with other designers and gain feedback on my work. If you’d like to get in touch, I’d love to hear from you! irenelue01@gmail.com
